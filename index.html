45-Day Product Development Roadmap

Immersive Gameplay · Learning Simulation

Roadmap Objective

The goal of this 45-day roadmap is to design, build, and validate a fully playable immersive learning simulation. The focus is on creating real gameplay systems, believable AI-driven NPC interactions, and a cohesive user experience that demonstrates how learning emerges through interaction inside a living game world.

45-Day Execution Plan

Days 1–10 · Core Gameplay Foundation

  • Finalize core gameplay loop (movement, interaction, survival mechanics)
  • Implement A1-level language interaction rules
  • Design NPC behavior logic (intent, mood, memory)
  • Collaborate with subject-matter experts (SMEs) to validate learning realism

Days 11–25 · Immersion & Interaction

  • Develop multiple in-game scenarios (café, market, street)
  • Integrate small LLM-driven NPC dialogue system
  • Improve visual feedback, animations, and environmental cues
  • Iterate gameplay based on internal testing and SME feedback

Days 26–35 · Experience Polish

  • Enhance UI clarity and in-world feedback systems
  • Optimize NPC conversation flow and adaptation logic
  • Create 2D → 3D transition and introduction video
  • Stabilize build for consistent demo experience

Days 36–45 · Validation & Demo Readiness

  • Playtest with early users and collect qualitative feedback
  • Measure engagement and session duration
  • Document technical and learning insights
  • Prepare live demo and public-facing materials
  • Launch
